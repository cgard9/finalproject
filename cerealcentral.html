Steps to Enjoy a Bowl of Cereal:

  1. Get a Bowl!
    • Find a bowl worthy of holding a bowl of tasty goodness.
    • It is important to not use a bowl fresh out of the dishwasher, as its warmth may cause the milk to go warm!
  2. Spoon
    • You're going to want a spoon that can deliver a good proportion of cereal to your mouth. Choose wisely!
  3. Milk
    • You've got some options. 1%, 2%, or Whole!
    • For you dairy-free people, try a good almond milk!
    • I don't recommend chocolate milk, but if combined with a chocolate cereal, you have my approval.
  4. Cereal
    • Put simply, eating cereal should not be a checkbox action. It is an art that needs to be appreciated and enjoyed.
    • Before any cereal goes into your mouth, smell it, gaze at it, and prepare yourself for happiness.
  5. Bon appetit!
